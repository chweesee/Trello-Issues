- Attempt to switch language 
- get a call, but don't accept it 
- switch language 
- let call run out 
- Sound will still be there

Sound of calling should dissapear after the call dissapears

The sound will still be there after you miss a call, even though it should normally dissapear. it can also be multiplied.
