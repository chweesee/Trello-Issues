Short Description: Images can be pasted into chats that disallow messages, the message will send but not appear in the channel
Steps to Reproduce:
1) Find channel that is read only
2) Screenshot something using ShareX or any other way to put an image on your clipboard
3) Ctrl+V into that channel
Expected Result: Pressing Ctrl+V in that channel shouldn't do anything
Actual Result: The image send confirmation box appears and 'upload' is clickable  but the image isn't actually sent in the channel
System Settings:  Windows 10 Discord Stable 1/08/16
Note: Could only get to reproduce on two servers
