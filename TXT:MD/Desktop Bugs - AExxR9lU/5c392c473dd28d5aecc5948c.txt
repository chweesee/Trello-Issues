####Steps to reproduce:
- Join a voice chat(in a server) 
- Go to settings > Voice settings 
- Turn on push to talk and make a shortcut 
- Go back to your call 
- Right click Settings 
- Go to voice and video 
- Hold down your push to talk bind as you click 'switch to voice activity' 
- Let go of your bind and click 'switch to Push to talk'  
- See how your voice is active when you're not holding your bind.

####Expected result:
The green ring around your name should work properly when you talk.
####Actual result:
The green ring around your name stays even when you're not holding your push to talk bind.

Canary 30702
