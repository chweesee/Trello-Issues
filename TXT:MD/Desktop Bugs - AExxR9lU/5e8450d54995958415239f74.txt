**Short Description:** Creating a server template and moving a channel to uncategorized does not sync in preview
**Steps to reproduce:**
-have a server with a channel in a category
-create a server template
-move the channel to uncategorized above all the channels
-go to server template in settings
-click sync
-press preview 

**Expected Results:** The channel syncs in preview, shows the channel above all channels in uncategorized
**Actual Results:** The channel does not sync in preview, and remains in the category that the template was first created with
**Client Settings:** Canary 56789 (fc465d8)
**System Settings:** Windows 10 64-Bit (10.0.18362)
