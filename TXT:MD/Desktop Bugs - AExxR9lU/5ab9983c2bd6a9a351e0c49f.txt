####Steps to reproduce:
- Make a new Discord or scroll up to the top of the first channel in a Discord 
-  Click the blue "share button" in the second row of text (Invite your friends) 
-  Press CTRL + K (CMD + K for OSX) to open the channel switcher 
- Press ESC to return to the share menu

####Expected result:
It returns to the share menu and is the same as it should be.
####Actual result:
You will now be able to type in the channel while the share menu is still visible.
####Client settings:
Discord Canary, 0.0.186, OSX
####System settings:
Mac High Sierra 10.3.3
