####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to a server where you are lacking manage channels in all, or only some channels. 
- Go to the channel settings for a channel where you have manage permissions, but not manage channel. 
- Observe the slider.

####Expected result:
You cannot drag the slider because you do not have permission to manage channels.
####Actual result:
The slider can be moved and shows the Save Changes option, but nothing is saved due to lacking permissions.

Canary 0.0.212 (Build 24815)
####System settings:
Windows 10 Pro (64-bit), Creators Update (v1709, Build 16299.492)
