Reported by Ene#0808

####Steps to reproduce:
- Open the Group DM Creation modal with ctrl + shift + t 
- Create a new Group DM with some friends 
- Open the Add Friends to DM modal inside of that Group DM and observe the invite field

####Expected result:
An invite should appear as opening that modal is suppose to automatically generate one
####Actual result:
The field appears empty, requirings you to click on the "Create" button to generate one. Furthermore, if you revoke that invite after creating it, it will stay in the invite field, making it impossible to generate a new valid invite for that Group DM
####Client settings:
Discord Canary 0.0.232, Build 32244
####System settings:
Windows 10 Home 64-bit (v1803)
