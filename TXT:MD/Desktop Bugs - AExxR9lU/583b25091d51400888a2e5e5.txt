####Steps to reproduce: 
- Be in a server/channel where you do not have the External emojis permission but have the reaction permission. 
- Try to use a super cool external emoji as a reaction to a message. 

####Expected result:
 There would be message telling you that you cannot react with external emojis. 
####Actual result:
 The emoji is used for a few seconds and then quickly disappears without a warning message. 
####System settings:
 Win 10, Latest Canary.
