Short Description:  Messages sent and deleted before you check a channel cause your reading position in the channel to jump up
Steps to Reproduce:
1)  View channel that isn't channel X
2)  Have person send a message to channel X, then delete it
3)  View channel X
Expected Result: viewing wherever you were viewing
Actual Result: You're scrolled back about 20-30 messages

Re-reporting this extremely annoying bug because I have no idea how this hasn't been fixed yet.
