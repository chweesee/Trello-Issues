Reported by Action Potential#1009

####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to any discord server. 
- Locate a person who has a nickname in that server. 
- Press on their user so you get that small pop up to appear. 
- Triple tap their real username. 
- You will see that it won't highlight their full username so that you can copy it. It will only highlight their name, not the hashtag. 
- Do the same thing on a user without a nickname and you will see that it highlights their username and hashtag. 
- Observe the video.

####Expected result:
When you tap on their name, it should highlight their username and hashtag.
####Actual result:
When you tap on their name, it only highlights the username part, without the hashtag.
####Client settings:
Canary 30151
####System settings:
MacOS 10.14.3
