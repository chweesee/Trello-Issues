####Steps to reproduce:-Hit up arrow, or right click to edit a message 
- Press CTRL+E

####Expected result:
A single emoji picker should open.
####Actual result:
Two emoji picker are opened, sometimes overlapping each other depending on the size of message being edited and its position in chat. The upper emoji picker adds emoji to the message being edited, with the lower emoji picker adding them to the text box.
####Client settings:
Canary, Build Number: 48134
####System settings:
Windows 10 Home (1903)
