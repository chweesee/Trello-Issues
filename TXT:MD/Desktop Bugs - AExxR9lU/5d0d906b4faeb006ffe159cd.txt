####Steps to reproduce:
- Minimize Discord (top right corner, looks like a dash or underscore). 
- In the Windows taskbar, right click Discord and click "Close window." 
- Once Discord is closed, try to reopen it by clicking on the Discord application.

####Expected result:
Discord should open minimized in the taskbar.
####Actual result:
Discord won't reopen and you'll have to restart your PC.

####System settings:
