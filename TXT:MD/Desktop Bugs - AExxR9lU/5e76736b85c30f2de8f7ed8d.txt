####Steps to reproduce:
- Open Discord (stable or canary) in Firefox 
- Put something on your clipboard 
- Click outside of Firefox so that Firefox loses focus 
- Right-click on the input box in Discord and choose Paste (paste might not be available the first time you right-click, so you may have to click out of Firefox and do it a second time)

####Expected result:
Contents of the clipboard should be pasted one time
####Actual result:
Contents of the clipboard are pasted twice

Release Channel: stable, Build Number: 56252, Version Hash: a0f34898c1c82aa63ce17b93829fb4c67a74720c
####System settings:
