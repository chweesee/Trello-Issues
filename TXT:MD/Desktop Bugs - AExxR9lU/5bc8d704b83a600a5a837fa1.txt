####Steps to reproduce:
- Run Fortnite, and go into the Battle Royale mode 
- Verify that you have a Rich Presence playing status for Fortnitr 
- Resfresh Discord using Ctrl + R 
- Observe your playing status when Discord reloads 
- Verify with other users to see if you have a playing status 
- Interact with the game in any way (Change game mode, join a party) and observe your playing status again

####Expected result:
When you refresh, Discord should show your status to everyone else
####Actual result:
Discord shows a local normal playing status for Fortnite, but no playing status to other users
