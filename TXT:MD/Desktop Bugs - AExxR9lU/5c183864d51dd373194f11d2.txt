####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to the Discord Store or the Activity menu or the Discord Library. 
- Use the "Create a private group" shortcut  (command + shift + t) or (control + shift + t) 
- Go to your friends tab.

####Expected result:
It should re-direct you to the "friends" menu, and open up the new group DM pop-up.
####Actual result:
It will crash your Discord, making you have to refresh it.

Discord Canary, Build 29791
