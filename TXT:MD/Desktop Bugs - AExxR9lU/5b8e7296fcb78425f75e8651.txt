####Steps to reproduce:
- Find a random user. 
- Make sure that "Show on Games Tab" is unchecked. 
- Block the user. 
- Right click them again, and click "Show on Games Tab". 
- The checkbox will not flip. 
- Unblock the user. 
- Right click again and observe the checkbox flipped.

####Expected result:
The checkbox should show flipping regardless if the user is blocked or not.
####Actual result:
You cannot see the checkbox flipping until you unblock them again.
####Client settings:
Canary 23018
####System settings:
MacBook Pro (13", Mid-2012), macOS 10.13.6 - Windows 10 Home (1803, x64)
