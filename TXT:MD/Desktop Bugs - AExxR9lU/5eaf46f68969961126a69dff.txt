Reported by Steets#0001

####Steps to reproduce:
- Write some text in the message box. 
- Before writing a word, press Ctrl+I or Ctrl+B to italicize or bold it. Double or quadruple asterisks are automatically inserted. 
- After writing a word, press Ctrl+I or Ctrl+B again to de-italicize or de-bold the text, as with any other word processor.

####Expected result:
The cursor is moved to the trailing asterisk, so typing can continue naturally whilst using keyboard shortcuts to format text.
####Actual result:
For italicizing, the last asterisk is deleted and the final letter of the word is highlighted. For bolding, the last two asterisks are deleted and the final two letters of the word are highlighted.
####Client settings:
Discord Canary Build 59390.
####System settings:
Windows 10 Enterprise 1909 64-bit (18363.815).
