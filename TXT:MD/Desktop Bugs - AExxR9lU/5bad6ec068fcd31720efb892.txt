Reported by Pascal#0929

####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to a channel with slowmode being enabled (set it to a bigger time interval to notice the bug) 
- Make sure you don't have manage channels or manage messages permission 
- Send a message 
- Observe the timer that appears now 
- Reload your client 
- Observe the timer/slowmode info in the channel again

####Expected result:
The slowmode timer should be shown after reloading the client.
####Actual result:
The timer disappears until you try to send a message again.
####Client settings:
Canary Build 24815
####System settings:
MacBook Pro 13" (2016), macOS Mojave 10.14
