####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to the language changer in settings 
- Change your language to non-English 
- Get ready to observe the "language" and "select a language" text at the top of the language changer panel 
- Change your language to a different non-English language 
- The "language" and "select a language" text should be English for a split second

####Expected result:
The "language" and "select a language" text should change between the 2 languages, without going to English first
####Actual result:
The "language" and "select a language" text changed to English first before changing to the new language
