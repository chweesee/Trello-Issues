####Steps to reproduce:
- Go into your Friends List 
- Go into your User Settings -> Appearance 
- Change your Show Games Tab preference to on or off (depending on what you had it set beforehand) 
- Exit out of your User Settings and observe

####Expected result:
The Games tab should correctly reflect your currently toggled setting for it without needing to switch to another area of Discord
####Actual result:
The Games tab will either not appear or still appear after changing your setting for it until you switch to another area of Discord
####Client settings:
Discord Canary 0.0.211, Build 23321
####System settings:
Windows 10 Home 64-bit (v1803)
