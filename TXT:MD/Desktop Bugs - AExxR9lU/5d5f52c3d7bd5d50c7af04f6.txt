####Steps to reproduce:
- Change a text channel with no slowmode to an announcements channel. 
- Confirm this in the audit log. (optional) 
- In the channel settings for the now announcements channel, uncheck the announcements channel toggle DO NOT SAVE YET. 
- Set the slowmode slider NO SAVING YET. 
- Check the announcements toggle again. Now you can save. 
- Open the audit log and look at the details for the channel modification.

####Expected result:
There should be no save button because there is no more slowmode slider visible anymore.
####Actual result:
There is a slowmode log in the audit log for the announcements channel even though slowmode doesn't work in announcements channels.
