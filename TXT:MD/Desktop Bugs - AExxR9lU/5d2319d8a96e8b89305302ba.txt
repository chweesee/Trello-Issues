Refreshing Discord without internet leads to a "softlock" blank screen and will not reconnect (https://trello.com/c/9Bx5Ijbl)

None

Created: 2019-07-08T10:24:24.586Z
Latest Update: 2020-07-16T21:26:04.408Z

Reported by Jman005#7755

####Steps to reproduce:
- Open Discord. 
- After it loads, disable your internet completely. 
- Press CTRL+R (or the equivalent of your device) to refresh Discord.  
- Reconnect to the internet.

####Expected result:
While without internet, a "connecting" screen should be present and after internet is enabled, Discord should reload.
####Actual result:
A blank screen appears which forces you to restart Discord.
####Client settings:
Release Channel: stable, Build Number: 40795
####System settings:
Windows 10 1709
