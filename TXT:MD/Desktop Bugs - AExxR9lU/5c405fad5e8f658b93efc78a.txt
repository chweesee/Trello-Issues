####Steps to reproduce:
- Enable 2FA on your account. 
- Open User Settings and click "View Backup Codes." 
- Enter your password. 
- Copy one of the backup codes. 
- Log out. 
- Sign back in using a backup code. 
- Switch to Dark theme if you haven't. 
- Open User Settings and click "View Backup Codes."

####Expected result:
The checkbox next to the used code should be in dark theme.
####Actual result:
The checkbox appears in light theme.

Build 30945
####System settings:
MacBook Pro (13", Mid-2012), macOS v10.14.2 (Mojave)
