Reported by VoidWhisperer#5905

####Steps to reproduce:
- Open overwatch or league 
- Open up the overlay 
- Right click out of the overlay to close it 
- Hit ESC to bring up a menu 
- Hit ESC a second time. The game should ignore the ESC until you click out of the game.

####Expected result:
Hitting ESC the second time should close the game menu
####Actual result:
ESC presses are ignored until the game loses and regains focus.
####Client settings:
Build 9631
####System settings:
Windows 10 Home x64
