####Steps to reproduce:
- Be on a server where there is an announcements channel that is set to NSFW. 
- Click the Follow button at the top. 
- Select a server and channel pair that are not tagged as NSFW. 
- Click "Follow".

####Expected result:
You are not able to follow the NSFW announcements channel to a non-NSFW channel.

You are able to follow NSFW announcements channels in non-NSFW channels which may potentially cause NSFW announcements to be published to the non-NSFW channel.
