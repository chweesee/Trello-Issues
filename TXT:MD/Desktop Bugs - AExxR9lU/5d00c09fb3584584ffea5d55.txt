None

Created: 2019-06-12T09:06:40.479Z
Latest Update: 2020-07-16T21:26:04.196Z

Reported by Rashaun#8467

####Steps to reproduce:
- Type ctrl shift f to switch to fullscreen mode 
- Click the maximise button 
- Type ctrl shift f to switch out of fullscreen mode 
- Click the maximise button again 
- Notice how it just switches Discord to fullscreen 
- Click the maximise button again 
- Notice how it goes back to normal 
- Minimise and reopen the window and then click the maximise button again

####Expected result:
Maximise button works properly and changes the size of the window
####Actual result:
Maximise button switches Discord in and out of fullscreen until window is minimised
####Client settings:
Canary Build 39515 Host 0.0.242
####System settings:
Windows 10 Home x64 1809
