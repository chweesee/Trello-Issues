####Steps to reproduce:
- Have 2 accounts in a server 
- Create a voice channel that requires a role to be seen 
- Join the voice channel as guild owner and move in your alt from another voice channel 
- Leave the server with your alt (while inside the voice channel) and rejoin

####Expected result:
The voice channel and it's contents should be hidden from view.
####Actual result:
The voice channel is still highlighted as if you're still inside it. You can view users inside it.
####Client settings:
Desktop Canary 0.0.245
####System settings:
Windows 10 Desktop x64 bit
