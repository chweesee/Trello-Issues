####Steps to reproduce:
- Find a random video on Youtube. 
- Open Discord, make sure you're on a text channel. 
- Ctrl+c , Ctrl+v the video URL to a text channel. 
- If you are using compact mode, change it to cozy. 
- After changing that, play the embedded Youtube video. 
- While playing the video, click on your avatar (the one that comes after sending a message, if you are unsure where to click, follow the GIF) to open the mini-profile. 
- Pause the Youtube video while your mini-profile is open. 
- Observe.

####Expected result:
Mini-profile closes after pausing the video.
####Actual result:
Mini-profile isn't closed after pausing the video.
####Client settings:
Desktop Canary 37031
####System settings:
Windows 7 Ultimate, 64-bit 6.1.7601, Service Pack 1
