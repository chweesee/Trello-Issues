####Steps to reproduce:
- Go into a channel 
- Jump to a message in the channel (can use search, pinned, or recent mentions) 
- Press the ESC key to jump back to the recent messages of the chat 
- Go into your User Settings -> Language and switch to another language then exit your User Settings 
- Observe that you're now jumped back to the previously jumped to message

####Expected result:
You're kept at the most recent messages as before you changed your client language
####Actual result:
You're jumped back to the previously jumped to message in chat
####Client settings:
Discord Canary 0.0.211, Build 18361
####System settings:
Windows 10 Home 64-bit (v1709)
