####Steps to reproduce:
- Have a lot of friends on Discord. 
- Open the invite modal for a server where you have permission to create invites. 
- Scroll down to the bottom of your friends list. 
- Click the "Invite" button next to someone at or near the bottom. 
- Scroll back to the top and look for the friend you invited.

####Expected result:
The button should be replaced with "Sent" as it normally does.
####Actual result:
After scrolling up, you're allowed to click the "Invite" button again.

Canary 31400
####System settings:
Windows 10 Home x64 (1809)
