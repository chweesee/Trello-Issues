Latest Update: 2018-10-22T20:16:57.771Z

Reported by Alex1304#9704

####Steps to reproduce:
- Grab a server in your server list with your mouse and hold it 
- Move it up or down to sort it. Do NOT release your mouse yet 
- While you're still holding it, press ESC

####Expected result:
The server will be moved back to its initial position in the server list (the sorting action is cancelled)
####Actual result:
the server will be placed at the position of your mouse cursor, exactly like if you released your mouse here
####Client settings:
Canary 6864
####System settings:
Ubuntu 16.04 LTS, Asus Netbook F551C, Intel Core i3 64bits
