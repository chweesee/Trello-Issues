####Steps to reproduce:
- Mention a voice channel (e.g #General) 
- Including text after it (e.g #General join me) 
- Outcome: (e.g #general join me) which is a text channel

####Expected result:
Discord shouldn't default the voice channel to a text channel if the names are the same when including text.
####Actual result:
Discord defaults voice channels to text channels when mentioning them if text is written after the mention
####Client settings:
Discord Stable latest version
####System settings:
Windows v.1809
