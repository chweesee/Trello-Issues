Discord's video player displays the length of a video found in metadata, not the actual length, causing some videos to display an invalid length (https://trello.com/c/PRUZzgxN)

None

Created: 2019-09-01T07:46:53.223Z
Latest Update: 2020-07-16T21:26:05.010Z

Reported by PintsizedSix40#5424

####Steps to reproduce:
- Upload videofile with incorrect length metadata (only tested with webm) 
- Watch video and look at time

####Expected result:
Discord's video player displays the actual length of a video
####Actual result:
Discord's video player displays the length of the video found in the metadata - (minus) the actual length for current time that is displayed, and the metadata length for the video length that is displayed. Firefox correctly displays the current length, although it is padded with zeros to match the broken video length. Chrome has the same result as Desktop.
####Client settings:
Build 44861 for desktop and browser.
####System settings:
Windows and Firefox
