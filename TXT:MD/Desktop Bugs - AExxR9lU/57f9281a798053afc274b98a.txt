Short Description: When editing a message, and right-clicking to mention someone, the mention will go into the edited message even if the cursor is focused on the new message bar.
Steps to Reproduce:

1) Send a message
2) Send another one
3) Right-click to edit your original message (#1)
4) Click the "Message #channel" bar
5) Right-click someone then click "Mention"
Expected Result: The mention goes into the new message bar
Actual Result: The mention pops up into the edit message box
