####Steps to reproduce:
- Ensure that clock is synchronized with remote user/internet time to within a second or two to prevent false positive results 
- Open Spotify 
- Open Discord 
- Click a user listening to spotify 
- Click listen along 
- Wait for the song to finish and the next song to start 
- There is a delay in the [Spotify] Listen along presence change: message that should pop up in the console,  and player:1 return 401 errors occasionally. 
- [Spotify] Listen along active but playback stopped or track changed. Stopping listen along in 5000ms messages occur normally.

####Expected result:
Music should continue smoothly as playlists progress from one song to another.
####Actual result:
There is silence for upwards of 30 seconds, followed by the song eventually starting.
####Client settings:
Desktop Stable 0.0.305 build 39974
####System settings:
Windows 10 Version 1803 (OS Build 17134.829) but also occurs on Windows 10 1903
