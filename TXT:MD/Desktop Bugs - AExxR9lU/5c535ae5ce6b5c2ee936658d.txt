####Steps to reproduce:
- Change your client language to a language that already has translated strings for the Gif Picker (e.g Spanish/Dutch) 
- Go into a Server/DM and click on the Gif Picker button 
- Observe the category names & suggestion buttons at the bottom of categories

####Expected result:
The category names & suggestion buttons should translate to your new client language

The category names & suggestion buttons stay in your previous client language, requiring you to refresh for the proper translation to apply
