####Steps to reproduce:
- Open up Discord 
- Load up a large server (Usually 10,000 or more) 
- Scroll to the bottom of the members tab 
- Hover over a user with your mouse
- When you move your mouse, your cursor will alternate from the hand icon to the cursor icon.

####Expected result:
Your cursor will show the hand icon meaning that you can click on the user
####Actual result:
Your cursor will display the hand icon for a couple frames and then reset to the regular cursor
####Client settings:
Desktop Canary, 30302
####System settings:
Windows 10, 1803
