Reported by Mason#1337

####Steps to reproduce:
- Enable developer mode 
- Mention a role in a chat channel with some text 
- Right click on the role mention and select "Copy ID" 
- Paste the copied text 
- Right click on the other text and select Copy ID 
- Paste the copied text (they are the same)

####Expected result:
Selecting "Copy ID" when right clicking a role mention should copy the role\_id to your clipboard
####Actual result:
The message\_id of the message in which the role was mentioned is copied to your clipboard
####Client settings:
Desktop Canary, 0.0.149
####System settings:
macOS Sierra 10.12.5
