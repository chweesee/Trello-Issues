Short Description: Canary only opens on 2nd monitor from minimized or maximized
Steps to Reproduce: 
   1 - 1) Move Discord over to monitor 1
   1 - 2) Minimize -> Open up

   2 - 1) Move Discord to monitor 1
   2- 2) Maximize -> un-maximize
Expected Result: It should pop up on the monitor it was minimized/maximized on
Actual result: It opens on monitor 2
Info: W10, Canary 0.0.134, 2x 1920x1080 monitors
