[MAC SPECIFIC] Turning off Hardware Acceleration in v39214 will make Discord unable to boot up during / after restart. (https://trello.com/c/mVTA7b8v)

None

Created: 2019-06-06T15:04:26.222Z
Latest Update: 2020-07-16T21:26:04.079Z

Reported by xTeal#7702

####Steps to reproduce:
- Be on a mac 
- Go to User Settings 
- Go to Appearance 
- Go to Advanced and on "Hardware Acceleration" 
- Turn off Hardware Acceleration 
- Tell Discord it can Restart 
- When Discord restarts and opens up, it has a white screen on the updater 
- Witness results & Crash 
- TO FIX : Go to Home > Library > Application Support > Delete Discordcanary folder and relaunch Canary.

####Expected result:
Discord will boot up normally, no blank screen on the updater screen.
####Actual result:
The updater screen is white, Discord Canary cannot boot up & crashes. Fix is listed in reproduce steps.
####Client settings:
Mac Discord Canary 39214
####System settings:
macOS Sierra 10.12.6 : Early 2015 Macbook Pro Retina
