####Steps to reproduce:
- Get in a call 
- open minecraft in windowed mode 
- screen share the minecraft window, you will see it on the screen share 
- Go to minecraft and press F11, the screen share turns black (make sure its full screen and not full screen windowed) 
- Stop the screen share 
- start a new screen share and look for the full screen minecraft, it isn't there

####Expected result:
The screen share should continue working
####Actual result:
The screen share turns black when entering windowed mode and sometimes you cannot locate a fullscreended window

Windows 10 pro, 64 bit
