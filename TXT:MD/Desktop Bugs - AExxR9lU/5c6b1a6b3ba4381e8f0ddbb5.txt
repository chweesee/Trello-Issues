####Steps to reproduce:
- Load up Discord.  
- Find someone in your Direct Messages list that is online, and that you know is on a mobile device. 
- Notice that you don't see the "Active on Mobile" tag right away. 
- Open a Discord server where you and that user and on. Find that user in the player database of the that server. 
- Notice that in the online list of the common server, the "Active on Mobile" tag does appear. 
- Go back to your Direct Messages list and observe that the "Active on Mobile" tag finally appears there.

####Expected result:
The "Active on Mobile" tag should appear as soon as Discord starts up.
####Actual result:
The "Active on Mobile" tag doesn't appear when Discord first starts up.
