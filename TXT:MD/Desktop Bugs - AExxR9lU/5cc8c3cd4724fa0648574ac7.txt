####Steps to reproduce:
- Set "show spoiler content" to always in text & images settings 
- Send a link in a spoiler 
- Click the link once 
- Notice how nothing happened (presumably the invisible spoiler is being affected) 
- Click the link again 
- Now it opens

####Expected result:
Because you can already see the link, you should only need to click it once.
####Actual result:
Even though you can already see the link, you have to click it twice to open it.
