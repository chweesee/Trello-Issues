Reported by Evan99w#6347

####Steps to reproduce:
- Have a group call with three or more people in
- Have a person say something in the chat 
- Remove that person from the group 
- Start a call with only yourself in (shift click call icon) 
- Right click on their name next to the message they sent 
- Select Ring, this will ring everyone

####Expected result:
The ring option in the menu should not be there as they are not in the group anymore
####Actual result:
It rings everyone in the group who is not already in the call

Release Channel: stable, Build Number: 36697, Version Hash: 71f9ee39150d6bb61291e6ae2cc500b79d63553c
####System settings:
Windows 10 Home Version 1809 (OS Build 17763. 437)
