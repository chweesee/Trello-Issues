Reported by weston#0001

####Steps to reproduce:
- Open Firefox and login to Discord 
- Press Ctrl + K to bring up the search box 
- Search for anything 
- Click anywhere in the box other than the search box and the search results (so click around the search box but not the search box itself or the results) 
- Try to press Escape to leave the menu 
- Notice it does not work

####Expected result:
When you push escape you should be able to leave the menu regardless of where you click
####Actual result:
You cannot leave the menu with Escape.
####Client settings:
Firefox Version 67.0, Discord Build: Release Channel: stable, Build Number: 38950
####System settings:
Windows 10 (64 bit) - 10.0.17134 Build 17134
