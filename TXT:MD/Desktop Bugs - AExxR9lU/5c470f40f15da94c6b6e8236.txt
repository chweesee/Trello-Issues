####Steps to reproduce:
- Have Audit Log access for any server. 
- Open Server Settings > Audit Log for that server. 
- Click "Filter by User," or click "Filter by Action." 
- Hover your cursor over your currently selected filter. Notice the cursor. 
- Click the filter.

####Expected result:
Nothing should happen since the cursor does not imply there is a button.
####Actual result:
You are able to select the current filter even though the cursor doesn't show a button there.
