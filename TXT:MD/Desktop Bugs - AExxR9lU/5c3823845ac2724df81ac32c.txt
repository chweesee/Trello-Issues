####Steps to reproduce:
- Make sure you currently have no gifs favorited 
- Set your client theme to White Theme 
- Go into a channel and open the Gif Picker 
- Go into the "Favorites" category and observe the lack of a darker grey around the hint cards as seen on Dark Theme 
- Scroll further down to the other hint cards and observe there being random horizontal streaks of white

####Expected result:
A darker grey surrounding for the hint cards like seen on Dark Theme and no horizontal streaks of white
####Actual result:
A lack of darker grey surrounding the hint cards and random horizontal streaks of white near the hint cards further down

Discord Canary 0.0.227, Build 30756
####System settings:
Windows 10 Home 64-bit (v1803)
