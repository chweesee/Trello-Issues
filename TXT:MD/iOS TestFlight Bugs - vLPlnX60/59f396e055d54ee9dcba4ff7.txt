####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to your iOS settings and disallow Discord access to the photos app 
- Open Discord again and search for an image/gif 
- Click on this image to open image preview 
- Click again on this image to open the buttons 
- Now click on the 3 dots 
- Try to save the image.

####Expected result:
You should get an error message that Discord doesn't have permission to your photos app (like for uploading photos).
####Actual result:
Right after clicking it Discord crashes.

TestFlight 1.9.3 (1340)
####System settings:
iPhone 6s with iOS 11.0.3 & iPad Air 2 with iOS 11.1 PB 5
