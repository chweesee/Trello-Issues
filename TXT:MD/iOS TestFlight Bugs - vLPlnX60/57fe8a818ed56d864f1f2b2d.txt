**Short Description:**  Can't re-drag highest Role back to highest after you start dragging it
**Steps to Reproduce:**
1)  in new iOS Roles screen, hit Edit, and start to move your highest role (tap and hold the hamburger, then move it down)
2)  Once it has gone below another role, while still dragging, try to put it back up to the top
**Expected Result:** the role should go back to the very tippity top
**Actual Result:** the role cannot go above the new highest role. you must let go of the drag, then drag again.

**System Settings:**  iOS 10.1 PB2 iPhone 6s+
