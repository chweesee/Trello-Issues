Reported by bryant#1011

####Steps to reproduce:
- Have iPad in landscape [Works in Portrait too] 
- Have Discord running in the background 
- Open a different app 
- Close the app via home button and open Discord 
- Observe the black bar that may appear [NOTE] the server list could slide open then close. However, if you don't get the black bar, continue below. 
- Open same or different app then switch to Discord via app switcher. 
- Black bar will appear, if it doesn't continue. [NOTE] Server list may flash open and close. 
- App switch to a different server, then app switch back to Discord. 
- Black bar should appear in location of member list.

####Expected result:
The member list should stay closed and not display a black bar. [NOTE] Server list should not flash open/close
####Actual result:
A black bar will appear on the right side or bottom, making the app unusable. [NOTE] The server list flashes open/close.
####Client settings:
TF 3.0.5(15973)
####System settings:
iPad 6th Gen' 9.7" '(iOS - 12.3.1)
