####Steps to reproduce:
- Make sure you have 2 accounts and iOS devices 
- Go the friends tab 
- Click on the add friends symbol 
- Search for nearby friends with both devices 
- Send a friend request 
- Ignore it via the pending tab 
- Close the friends tab 
- Open it again and search for nearby friends 
- Observe the send button

####Expected result:
It will give you the option to send a second friend request.
####Actual result:
It still shows a "Sent" button with no option to send another friend request.
####Client settings:
TestFlight 2.3.7 (6914)
####System settings:
iPhone 8 Plus, iPad Air 2 - iOS 12 Public Beta 8
