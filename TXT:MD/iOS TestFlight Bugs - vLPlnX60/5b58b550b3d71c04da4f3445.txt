####Steps to reproduce:
- Create an rich embed with a bot or webhook 
- Add a thumbnail to it 
- Try it with and without title link 
- Click on the thumbnail with your iOS device

####Expected result:
Clicking on the thumbnail should always open the image preview of it.
####Actual result:
It opens the image in your selected browser. If you have a title link for the embed, it opens this link.
