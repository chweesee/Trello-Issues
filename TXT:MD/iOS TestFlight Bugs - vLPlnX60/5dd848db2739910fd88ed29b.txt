Reported by Ult1mat3#8838

####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to “Server Settings” by tapping on the 3 dots on your own discord server 
- Tap the “Channels” option 
- Tap the “Edit” button near the voice/text channels you want to edit 
- Tap “Close” on the top left  
- Try tapping the “Sort” button in the top right, choose either Categories, Text Channels or Voice Channels, then attempt to drag and drop the channels in the order you want them to be in

####Expected result:
You would be allowed to freely rearrange the channels to your liking
####Actual result:
No longer have the ability to drag and drop channels, they just show as being selected but don’t actually move
####Client settings:
TestFlight 3.1.7 (18209)
####System settings:
iPod Touch 6th Gen, iOS 12.4.3
