Reported by Kuuhaku#4503

####Steps to reproduce:
- Log out of Discord 
- Go to Settings > General > Accessibility > VoiceOver and turn on VoiceOver 
- Go back to Discord and tap on `Login` 
- Attempt to tap on the blue `Forgot your password?` text

####Expected result:
After double tapping on the text, a pop-up should appear, as it does with VoiceOver disabled
####Actual result:
Nothing happens, the pop-up doesn't appear
####Client settings:
TestFlight Build 2.4.0 (8921)
####System settings:
iOS 12.0.1
