[Archived] When editing a role's name, pressing the backspace button on the virtual keyboard can cause letters to re-appear. (https://trello.com/c/AKhhddgl)

None

Created: 2016-10-05T18:24:24.315Z
Latest Update: 2016-10-14T23:51:16.468Z

Steps to Reproduce:

1)  Progress to the Roles menu.
2)  Create a new role.
3)  Select that role.
4)  Tap on the Role name text entry field.
5)  Press and hold the backspace key on the virtual keyboard until all of the letters have been erased.
6)  Let go of the backspace key.

Buggy event:  A single character reappears.
Note:  Typing after this, a letter will randomly get deleted.
Expected:  Letters should only be removed/added deliberately.
