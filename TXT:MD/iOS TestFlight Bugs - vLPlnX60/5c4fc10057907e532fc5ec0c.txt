####Steps to reproduce:
- Put a spoiler tag in a channel topic 
- Open the member list and long press the spoiler tag 
- Move your finger far away from the spoiler tag, and release 
- Observe the spoiler tag after releasing your finger

####Expected result:
Spoiler doesn't get revealed because your finger was moved off the spoiler tag
####Actual result:
Spoiler gets revealed even though you weren't on the spoiler tag anymore
####Client settings:
TestFlight 2.4.3 (10936)
####System settings:
iPhone 6s iOS 12.2 Dev Beta
