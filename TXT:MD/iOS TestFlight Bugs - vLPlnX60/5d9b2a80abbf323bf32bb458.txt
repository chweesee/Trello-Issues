####Steps to reproduce:
- Open another app that is not Discord 
- Swipe up from the bottom slightly to open recent apps 
- Hold down Discord until you can drag it, then drag it into the center of your screen and let go 
- At this point, Discord should be open with the other app in the background. Go to a channel, and open your keyboard to type a message 
- Observe the message input box

####Expected result:
Discord should push the input box above the keyboard
####Actual result:
The input box does not get pushed up above the keyboard and gets slightly cut off by the keyboard
####Client settings:
TestFlight 3.1.4 (17575)
####System settings:
iPad Pro 11" (3rd Gen) on iPadOS 13.1.2
