####Steps to reproduce:
- On iOS, open a DM and tap the text field to open the keyboard 
- On another device (e.g. Desktop), open the same DM and start a call with that user 
- On iOS, observe that the text field has expanded in size 
- Tap on the text area and then back on the field to reset its size 
- Get the user to leave the call 
- Observe that the text field has disappeared

####Expected result:
The text field should remain the same size
####Actual result:
The text field either expands or disappears
