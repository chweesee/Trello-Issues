####Steps to reproduce:
- Open a text channel 
- Scroll up until the jump to present button appears 
- Start scrolling slowly 
- While still holding your finger on the screen, tap the jump to present button (may take a couple taps) 
- One the channel scrolls back down, move the finger you were holding on the screen 
- Observe snap back to previous position

####Expected result:
The channel should start scrolling from the beginning of the channel.
####Actual result:
The channel jumps back to the previous scrolling spot.
