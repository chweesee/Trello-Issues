####Steps to reproduce:
- User with "Connect" permissions in a channel join this channel, but YOU don't have "View Channel" permissions for this channel. Also your permission of “Connect” to this channel should be set to “/“, not to “X”. 
- You click on profile of this user and click "Join Voice" button. 
- Now you can see this voice channel and it's category until restarting Discord app.

####Expected result:
You should see current voice channel of a user if you don't have "View Channel" permissions of his current channel.
####Actual result:
You see channel without "View Channel" permissions.
