####Steps to reproduce:
- Activate an AFK channel through server settings 
- Change the timeout from the default to another time 
- Change the AFK channel back to no channel

####Expected result:
The timeout should remain the time that had been set
####Actual result:
The timeout resets back to five minutes. This is most likely a visual bug because after re-activating the AFK channel, the timeout setting goes back to the custom one that had been set.
####Client settings:
TestFlight 3.1.9 (18507)
####System settings:
iPhone 7s, iOS 13.1.3
