Latest Update: 2020-02-15T14:08:28.822Z

Reported by Pascal#0929

####Steps to reproduce:
- Long hold any message to open the action sheet 
- Rotate your iPad (1 or 2 times needed, depending on your initial orientation) 
- Observe the message action sheet

####Expected result:
The message action sheet should keep the same size when rotating your iPad.
####Actual result:
Depending on the amount of actions that is shown in the action sheet it can completely disappear or just shrink a bit.
####Client settings:
TestFlight 3.1.4 (17575)
####System settings:
iPad Pro (11-inch), iPadOS 13.1.2
