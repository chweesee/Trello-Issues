####Steps to reproduce:

- Have your iPhone’s Appearance on Dark Mode in your System Settings 
- Open Discord 
- Go to Discord’s Settings > Appearance > Light Mode 
- Go to either your friend list, a Direct Message, and/or on a server channel (where you chat, either works) 
- Scroll 
- Observe

####Expected result:
You see the Gray scroll color for Discord’s Light Mode and the phone’s Systems Settings Dark Mode.
####Actual result:
You don’t see the gray scroll color in Discord’s Light Mode and the phone’s System Settings in Dark Mode which makes it difficult to know where the scroll is when you are scrolling.
####Client settings:
TestFlight 3.1.4 (17575)
####System settings:
iPhone 8, 13.1.2
