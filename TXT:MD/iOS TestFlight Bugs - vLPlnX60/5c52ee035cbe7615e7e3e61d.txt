####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to Discord settings, change Web Browser setting to In-app browser. 
- Attach an image. Do not send it. 
- Make sure the keyboard is currently visible. 
- Click the image in the pending uploads bar. 
- Click on the image and select the three dots menu on the top right. 
- Select Open in Browser. 
- Leave the browser.

####Expected result:
The keyboard reopens.
####Actual result:
The keyboard is gone, but empty space occupies the keyboard area.
####Client settings:
Discord TestFlight 2.4.4 (11030)
####System settings:
iPhone XS Max, iOS 12.0.1

### **Extension to the issue**

- Make sure you have kick or ban members permission
- Open any channel
- Click on the message bar so the keyboard opens
- Long hold a user
- Click on user settings
- Select Kick or Ban
- Open the keyboard again by clicking on the reason field
- Close it and go back to the chat

**Another extension to the issue**

- Write any text
- Select the text
- Click on "Look Up" or the "Share" option

**Another extension to this issue (iPad only)**

- Go to a server
- Open Discord in Split View mode
- Go to any place in the other app where you can open the keyboard with one click
- Open the keyboard in Discord by clicking on the message field
- Now open the keyboard in the other app in Split View mode with Discord
- Swipe left or right in Discord to open member or channel list
- Close the keyboard

**Another Extension to this issue (iPad only)**

- Open any other app in Slide Over mode with Discord
- Remove the other app from Slide Over mode (by swiping the app to the right)
- Now go to any text channel and click on the message field to open the keyboard
- Swipe from the bottom a bit up to open the iPad dock
- Click on the app you have recently opened in Slide Over mode to open it again in Slide Over mode
