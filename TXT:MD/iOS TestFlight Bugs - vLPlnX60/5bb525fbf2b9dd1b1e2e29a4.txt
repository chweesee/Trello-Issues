Reported by Rashaun#9295

####Steps to reproduce:
- Go into user settings on your phone, and go to appearance 
- Toggle off the "Sync across clients" option 
- Go into appearance settings on your computer and change your theme 
- Verify that your theme didn't change on your phone 
- Toggle Developer Mode off/on from your computer, and observe the toggle on your phone

####Expected result:
Developer Mode toggle doesn't change because "sync across clients" is turned off
####Actual result:
Developer Mode can be toggled on/off despite having "sync across clients" turned off
####Client settings:
TestFlight 2.3.9 (7858) - Canary Build 25325
####System settings:
iPhone 6s iOS 12.1 Dev Beta 2 - Windows 10 Home x64 1803
