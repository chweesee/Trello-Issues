####Steps to reproduce:

- Create a category with at least 2 channels in it (e.g. channel `Test` and `Test2`

- Collapse the category
- Long press channel `Test2` to get a new modal window and click "Mark as read"

####Expected result:
The channel should disappear when getting marked as read.
####Actual result:
The channel stays there
