####Steps to reproduce:
- Have a server with at least three categories with several channels each 
- Have a channel in the top category have new messages 
- Mute the second category but make sure it has unread channels 
- Be in a channel on the bottom category 
- Scroll until the unread channel in the top category is out of view 
- Collapse the muted category 
- Observe the top of the channel list

Nothing happens as the unread channel comes into view
####Actual result:
After the unread channel comes into view, the new unreads banner appears the disappears
