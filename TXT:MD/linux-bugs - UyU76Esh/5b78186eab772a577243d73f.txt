####Steps to reproduce:
- Reboot Ubuntu 18.04 with Discord installed 
- Enter a voice chat 
- Voices are full of static and difficult to hear

- restart Discord 
- all voices are audible and static is gone

The audio is unclear and full of static until the Hardware Acceleration setting is toggled
