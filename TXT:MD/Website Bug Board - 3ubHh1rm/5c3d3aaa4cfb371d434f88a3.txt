####Steps to reproduce:
- Navigate to the dev portal. 
- Open the "Teams" tab. 
- Create a team. 
- Open the "Applications" tab. 
- Select the team you just created as the active applications list. 
- Open the "Teams" tab once more. 
- Delete the team you created. 
- Finally, open the "Applications" tab once again.

####Expected result:
The tab should default to either showing your personal applications, or prompt to select a team/your personal applications.
####Actual result:
The tab just shows an empty application list with the text on the dropdown cut off.
