####Steps to reproduce:
- Be an invited admin (not an owner) of a team 
- Go to the team developer console (https:// discordapp . com / developers / teams) 
- Open the team you are an admin of 
- Observe how the "invite team members" form is not disabled 
- Type a username into the "invite team members" form, and submit it 
- Observe how you are not allowed to invite other team members

The "invite team members" form should have been disabled, because you can't do anything with it
####Actual result:
The "invite team members" form is enabled, and you only find out you can't add new team members when you actually try to submit it
