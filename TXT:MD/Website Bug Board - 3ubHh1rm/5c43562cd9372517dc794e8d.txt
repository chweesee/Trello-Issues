When signing up for an account through the homepage and you uncheck the GDPR box before solving the captcha. Discord will error out with `Please update Discord to continue.`. (https://trello.com/c/26xvrJXA)

Reported by Isabel#0002

####Steps to reproduce:
- Be in a country with GDPR. (You can use a VPN to achieve this.) 
- Go to Discord's homepage. 
- If you're currently signed in, sign out then return to the homepage. 
- Click "Open Discord in your browser." 
- Enter a username, and check the box stating you've read the terms. 
- When the Captcha appears, uncheck the terms box. 
- Solve the Captcha.

####Expected result:
The site should prompt to check the box.
####Actual result:
`Please update Discord to continue.`
####Client settings:
Build 31082 - Firefox v64.0.2 (64-bit)
####System settings:
MacBook Pro (13", Mid-2012), macOS v10.14.2 (Mojave)
