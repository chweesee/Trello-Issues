####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to the Store tab 
- Click "View All Games" 
- Open your User Settings -> Language and change your client language 
- Exit out of your User Settings and observe the header buttons on the top left

####Expected result:
The buttons should translate to your new client language
####Actual result:
The buttons stay in your previous client language, requiring you to refresh for the proper translation to apply

Discord Canary 0.0.229, Build 31441
####System settings:
Windows 10 Home 64-bit (v1803)
