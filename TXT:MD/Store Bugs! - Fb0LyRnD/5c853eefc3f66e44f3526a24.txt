Latest Update: 2019-06-21T20:47:14.872Z

Reported by TheForbiddenAi#7549

####Steps to reproduce:
- Have a DLC in your library from the featured games list(Ex: Voidborne Onslaught, that one is currently free) 
- Restart discord 
- Go to the store tab 
- Go to the feature games list 
- Find the DLC in there 
- Observe how it shows as if you can click on the add to library button (It will silently fail) 
- Click on the DLC 
- Observe how you can't click on it 
- Go back to the featured games list and find the DLC 
- Observe how you can't click on the add to library button

####Expected result:
The add to library button should show as if you can't click on it
####Actual result:
The add to library button shows as if you can click on it until you click on the DLC
####Client settings:
Canary 33683
####System settings:
Windows 10 Home x64 (1809)
