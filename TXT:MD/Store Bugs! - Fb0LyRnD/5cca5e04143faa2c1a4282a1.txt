Rating text on game pages doesn't adapt initially after changing your client language (https://trello.com/c/hfNSIN6t)

None

Created: 2019-05-02T03:03:32.464Z
Latest Update: 2019-06-04T21:51:05.046Z

Reported by Ene#0808

####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to the Nitro tab 
- Click on a game that has the rating label at the bottom (e.g. Hob) 
- Open your User Settings -> Language and change your client language 
- Exit out of your User Settings, scroll back down to the rating label and observe the text next to it

####Expected result:
The text should translate to your new client language
####Actual result:
The text stays in your previous client language, requiring you to refresh for the correct translation to apply
####Client settings:
Discord Canary 0.0.235, Build 36697
####System settings:
Windows 10 Home 64-bit (v1809)
