Reported by The Lunala#7777

####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to Settings>Behaviour and select back button opens channel drawer if you haven't already. 
- Get out of settings and go to a channel(say channel A of server S). 
- Click on the back button twice to open the quick switcher. 
- Navigate to any channel in a different server(say channel B of server T). 
- Now, open channel drawer and navigate to any channel other than the channel you started with (channel A in our case) and click on a jump link in that channel.

####Expected result:
You get navigated to the channel just fine.
####Actual result:
The initial channel (channel A) flashes for a second before you get navigated.
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 867
####System settings:
Lenovo Vibe K5+, 5.1.1
