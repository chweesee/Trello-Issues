####Steps to reproduce:
- Open Discord to a channel where you have the manage channel permission on your phone and one other device 
- Open the touch keyboard on your phone and switch to the numbers and symbols view 
- On the other device, change the channel name or topic

####Expected result:
The keyboard should not be reset and you should be able to continue typing your message without interruption
####Actual result:
The keyboard resets, switching back to the default view. This also breaks autocorrect and sometimes moves the cursor.
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 6.6.1 (661)
####System settings:
Samsung Galaxy S9 - Android 8.0 Oreo
