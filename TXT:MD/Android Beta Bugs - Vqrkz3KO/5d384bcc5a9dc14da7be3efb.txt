####Steps to reproduce:
- Press-hold on a folder. 
- Move it and place it exactly above another folder. 
- Release your finger. 
- Observe the folder go back to its spot.

An empty folder is created as soon as you release the folder that you were press-holding. That empty folder just disappears (replaced by the original folder) when your original folder repositions and goes back to its original spot.
