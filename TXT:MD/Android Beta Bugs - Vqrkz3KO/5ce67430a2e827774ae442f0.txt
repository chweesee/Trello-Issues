####Steps to reproduce:
- Find a verified server with a banner (for example DTesters) 
- Scroll down in the channel list until the banner disappears completely. 
- Switch to a normal server (which doesn't have a banner) 
- Observe the channel list animation.

####Expected result:
The channel list animation should only have the quick fade-in channel animation.
####Actual result:
After the quick fade-in animation the drawer also does a slide-in from the top animation. (It looks like the channel drawer loads twice)

Discord Alpha 9.0.2
####System settings:
Nokia 6, Android 9.
