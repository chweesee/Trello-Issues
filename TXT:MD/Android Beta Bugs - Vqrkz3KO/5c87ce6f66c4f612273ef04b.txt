####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to Settings > Text & Images 
- Disable "When uploaded directly to Discord", but keep "When sent as links" enabled 
- Post a link embedding an image 
- Observe

####Expected result:
Images from embed links should be immune to the "When uploaded directly to Discord" option being disabled
####Actual result:
Image previews from links are still disabled despite "When sent as links" being enabled. This creates weird side effects such as messing up YouTube embeds (see screenshot)
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 8.5.6
####System settings:
Huawei Mate 9 Android 8.0
