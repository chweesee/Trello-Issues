####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to a server where you have the Create Instant Invite permission and press the Create Instant Invite Button at the top of the channel list 
- Click the wheel icon right next to the invite link and switch to landscape mode 
- Try closing the settings menu by swiping down and notice that you need to do it twice instead of only once like in portrait mode

####Expected result:
One swipe down should close the settings menu like in portrait mode
####Actual result:
Two swipe downs are required to close the settings menu

Discord Alpha 4.1.3 (509)
####System settings:
Samsung Galaxy S8 (SM-G950F), Android Nougat 7.0
