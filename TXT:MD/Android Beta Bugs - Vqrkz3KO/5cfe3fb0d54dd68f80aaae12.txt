Latest Update: 2019-08-16T21:24:14.406Z

Reported by Alex1304#9704

####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to a channel you can type in 
- In the message box, type anything, for example "test". Don't send it, leave it in the message box 
- Highlight the text by long pressing it, as if you wanted to copy it 
- While text is in highlight, open emoji picker 
- Insert an emoji

####Expected result:
The text you highlighted should be overwritten and replaced by the emoji
####Actual result:
The text doesn't get erased, the emoji is appended to the end of the selection
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 9.0.9
####System settings:
Huawei Mate 9 Android 9.0
