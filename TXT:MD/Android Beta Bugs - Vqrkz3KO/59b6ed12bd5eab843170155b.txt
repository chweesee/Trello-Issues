####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to someone's profile 
- Type a note 
- Without touching any other part of screen, press the back button to "close" the keyboard  
- Press the recent apps button then close the app 
- Reopen the app, go to user's profile, and observe: the note was not saved

####Expected result:
The notes should save automatically, without needing to continue using the app/doing an action/closing the profile
####Actual result:
The notes doesn't save if you don't do any action such as closing the profile, checking mutual
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 5.1.4
####System settings:
Android 4.4.4, Tablet Allview Viva H8 LTE
