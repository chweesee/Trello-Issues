####Steps to reproduce:
- Disable the Discord-App notifications from your android app-settings. 
- Go and do any action that could result in receiving a toast message (e.g: Create a server with a name of more than 100+ characters, or Join a server when you are already at 100 servers). 
- Let's do the server name thing; type a name with 100+ chars 
- Click on "Create" at the top right corner, in order to create the server.

####Expected result:
An error message, known as Toast, should pop up and tell you why is the server not being created. (Server names with 100+ chars are not allowed).
####Actual result:
Nothing happens, the app just loads and then puts you back to server creation page.

Discord 888
