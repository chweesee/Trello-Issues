####Steps to reproduce:
- On the desktop/android app and in a Discord server which you have Manage Roles permission, go to Server Settings 
- Under User Management, go to Members 
- Tap on a member whose highest role is the same as yours

####Expected result:
You would not be allowed to modify their roles
####Actual result:
In the android app, you will not be permitted to do so but in Discord desktop, you are able to.
####Client settings:
Discord Canary 0.0.163
####System settings:
Windows 10 Pro x64, Discord 4.10 (505) Zenfone 5 Android 5.0
