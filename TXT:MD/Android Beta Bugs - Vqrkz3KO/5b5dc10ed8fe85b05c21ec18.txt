####Steps to reproduce:
- Switch to light theme. 
- Join a voice channel. 
- Open a text channel. 
- Close keyboard. 
- Observe the voice bar.

####Expected result:
You see the channel name below the "Voice Connected" text.
####Actual result:
You just see an empty space below it. If you change your theme to dark, you see the channel name below the text. The Colour of the text of the voice channel is exactly same as the colour of the voice bar on light theme.

Discord Alpha 720
####System settings:
