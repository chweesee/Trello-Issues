[Archived] When you move the cursor to the other side of the number in the enter 2FA field of the login page and hit backspace, the cursor jumps to the box to the right of the box you were in. (https://trello.com/c/JQKXluOx)

Reported by The Lunala#7777

####Steps to reproduce:
- Enabled 2FA on your account. 
- Logout. 
- Login again. 
- After entering the password and clicking login, you'll be taken to the 2FA code page. 
- Start filling in random numbers in the boxes (don't fill all 6; fill maybe like 3 to 4). 
- Now, move the cursor to the other side of the box (i.e If the cursor is on the the right side of the number in the box, move it to the left of the number but keep it in the same box. 
- Hit backspace.

####Expected result:
The numbers to the left of the cursor is deleted (i.e. the number in the box left of the box you selected).
####Actual result:
The cursor jumps to the box right of the box you selected.
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 848
####System settings:
Lenovo Vibe K5+, 5.1.1
