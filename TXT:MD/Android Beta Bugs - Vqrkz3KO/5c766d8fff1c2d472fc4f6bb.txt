####Steps to reproduce:
- Connect your Spotify account to Discord. 
- Uninstall Spotify from your device (if you had it). 
- Open Settings>Connections. 
- Click on the button that redirects to the Spotify app settings (the box with the arrow pointing out of the top right corner).

####Expected result:
You get some kind of error message.
####Actual result:
It just fails silently.
