- Make sure your Discord account linked to Game Launcher 
- Open a game like PUBG or Minecraft 
- Make sure it's showing your status on Discord

The game status should disappear as the game was closed

Samsung Galaxy A50, Android 9.0 Pie, One UI 1.1
