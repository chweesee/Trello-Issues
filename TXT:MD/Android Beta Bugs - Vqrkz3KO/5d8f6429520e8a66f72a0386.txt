Reported by Alex1304#9704

####Steps to reproduce:
- Type a short sentence ending with an emote and a `.` (or a `'`) immediately following the emote, without space 
- Edit the message, and add random characters to the beginning of the line until the emote is pushed to the very right edge of the line. Might require several edits to be as precise as possible.

####Expected result:
The emote should never duplicate itself
####Actual result:
The emote duplicates itself to next line
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 9.6.2
####System settings:
Huawei Mate 9 Android 9.0
