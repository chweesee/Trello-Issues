[Archived] Uploading a PNG emoji will save a copy of it with a JPG extension in your phone storage, even though it's actually a png (https://trello.com/c/B3Gmu2TF)

P3 - Will be fixed eventually,
Mid

Created: 2019-02-23T07:06:06.379Z
Latest Update: 2019-06-10T20:43:09.704Z

Reported by Alex1304#9704

####Steps to reproduce:
- Server Settings > Emoji 
- Upload emoji 
- Select a PNG image (preferably with a transparent background for more evidence) 
- Once the emoji has been uploaded, close Discord and go to your file explorer 
- Navigate to `[Local Storage]/Pictures/Discord/` 
- Find the emoji you just uploaded

####Expected result:
It should have a PNG extension
####Actual result:
The file has a JPG extension, even though it's actually a PNG as you can notice it does have the transparent background, which is impossible for an actual JPG image.
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 8.5.0
####System settings:
Huawei Mate 9 Android 8.0
