Reported by Bullseye#8200

####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to a server where you have admin permissions. 
- Open server settings. 
- Turn off your wifi / mobile data. 
- Open the Overview settings, then switch to landscape. 
- When in landscape, turn your wifi back on, then directly switch back to portait.

####Expected result:
When your wifi is off, the server name field says: "Server Name". Then when the wifi is back on, that field updates and contain your actual server name.
####Actual result:
The field does not update due to switching oriantation, and contains the default text.
####Client settings:
Alpha 909
####System settings:
Huawei Y7, Android 8.0.0
