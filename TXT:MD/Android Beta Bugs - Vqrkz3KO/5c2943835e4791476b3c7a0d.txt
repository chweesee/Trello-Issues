Reported by ginkoid#8888

####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to Android Settings > Google > Nearby > Settings cog, and make sure Discord is off 
- Restart Discord 
- Go to Friends > Add Friends, and tap the "start nearby scanning" button 
- When the Nearby permission modal opens (it might take a while for it to open), tap "deny" 
- Observe that Discord thinks it's still scanning, even though it doesn't have the permission to do so

####Expected result:
Discord Nearby scanning should stop
####Actual result:
Discord continues to think it's scanning
####Client settings:
8.2.2
####System settings:
Pixel 3 XL, Android 9 PQ1A.181205.006, Discord 8.2.2
