####Steps to reproduce:
- Read the steps carefully it's easy to make a mistake 
- You need two users for this bug say A and B  
- From A, send any message to a channel/DM 
- Again From A, Press enter making a new line then insert an emote inside a spoiler (please note that there should be no spaces separating the emote from the spoiler ) 
- From B, send an emote  
- From B, reveal the spoiler

####Expected result:
The unused new line should not be visible while revealing the spoiler
####Actual result:
The new line is visible and emote is revealed on a new line, but upon re-revealing the spoiler (switching to another channel and coming back) the line disappears
####Client settings:
Alpha 8.5.5
####System settings:
ASUS Zenfone max pro m1 Android 8.1.0
