[Archived] Typing out an invalid gift code and clicking the checkmark button on your keyboard (so that the error appears) and then editing the code then clicking the checkmark again (while the error is still there) causes the text field to cut the loading icon. [Small screens specific (5 inches)]. (https://trello.com/c/VbHfozj8)

P4, unlikely to ever be fixed.

Created: 2019-08-25T08:09:23.132Z
Latest Update: 2020-02-20T21:33:20.861Z

Reported by The Lunala#4308

####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to Settings>Nitro Gifting. 
- Type in random characters in the text field there and hit the checkmark on your keyboard. 
- Click on the text field again and type more random characters (don't clear the characters you've already written). 
- Hit the checkmark button again. 
- Observe the loading icon in the right side of the text field.

####Expected result:
The loading icon doesn't overlap with the text field.
####Actual result:
It overlaps the text field.

Discord Alpha 947
####System settings:
Lenovo Vibe K5+, 5.1.1
