Reported by Logiz#9321 (Original LunarSkies™#9575)

####Steps to reproduce: 
- Make a user with manage roles 
- Have the user open the manage channel permissions on android 
- Tap permissions 
- add user/role permissions to channel 

####Expected result:
  User has permissions given to them and cannot override 
####Actual result:
  User can override granting them the ability to delete channels System Settings: Android 6.0 System settings: Android 6.0 Client version: 3.1.8
