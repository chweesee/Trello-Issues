####Steps to reproduce:

- Go to your server add open someone's mini profile. 
- Click on the ban button in the mini profile. 
- Observe that the mini profile remains open behind the ban modal. 
- Now, close the ban modal. 
- Open the kick modal and observe the mini profile closes behind the kick modal.

####Expected result:
Either the profile closes in both cases or remains open in both cases.
####Actual result:
The mini profile closes in case of kick but remains open in case of ban.
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 844
####System settings:
Lenovo Vibe K5+, 5.1.1
