**Short Description:** when you have two channels #wikia and #wikia-vstf , mentioning #wikia-vstf will only linkify #wikia instead. 
**Step to reproduce:**  
1. type #wkia-vstf in a server with both #wikia and #wikia-vstf, 
2. select #wikia-vstf, 
3. send. 
**Expected result:** link #wikia-vstf actual result:#wikia get linked, -vstf become raw text. Beta v3.0.2.
