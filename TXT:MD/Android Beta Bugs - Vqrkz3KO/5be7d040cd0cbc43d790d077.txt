####Steps to reproduce:
- Get an Android device 
- Set your Appearance for Discord from Dark to Light 
- Take a picture/video/or have a file 
- Go to your file manager or something (I have a Samsung device, so just go to My Files) 
- Find an option to share it 
- Select Discord in the Share list 
- You will see the Message Preview 
- Select a random user 
- Check the USER:DISCRIMINATOR 
- It is really hard to read

####Expected result:
The USER:DISCRIMINATOR is supposed to be easy to read
####Actual result:
The USER:DISCRIMINATOR is really hard to read

Discord 7.8.8 (788) [I think it's Beta, Google Play says that]
####System settings:
Samsung Galaxy J7, Android 7.1.1
