Short Description: Channel and user list don't update on joining a new server.
Steps to Reproduce: ...
1) Open the a private chat (can be a regular chat, but its easier to demonstrate)
2) join a server using an invite link.
3) Swipe right or left and observe.
Expected Result: Shows channel list for server and user list
