When you disable the "Use data to improve Discord" and "Use data to customise my Discord experience" and change any other setting in the page, the two options enable themselves against. (https://trello.com/c/otcGHkzl)

P1 - Severe

Created: 2019-02-20T15:40:51.321Z
Latest Update: 2019-02-26T18:20:45.601Z

Reported by The Lunala#6877

####Steps to reproduce:
- Login to your alt account (because doing this on your main account wouldn't be a good idea). 
- Open Settings>Privacy & Safety. 
- Disable the "Use data to customise my Discord experience" and/or "Use data to improve Discord" options. 
- Make changes to any other settings in the page (like changing who can add you as friend, who can DM you, whom to scan DMs from). 
- Scroll down. 
- Observe the options you just disabled.

####Expected result:
They remain disabled.
####Actual result:
They enable themselves.
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 848
####System settings:
Lenovo Vibe K5+, 5.1.1
