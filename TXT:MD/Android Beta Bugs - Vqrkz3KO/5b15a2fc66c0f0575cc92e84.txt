####Steps to reproduce:
- Send a message 
- Tap the manifying glass 
- Search for the message you just sent and jump to it. Note that it works for any messages, even an older one. As long as you jump to something 
- Long press your message and Edit it 
- You can start editing your message, don't send it yet 
- Press the Home button of your device  
- Go back to Discord using the icon in your homescreen (not from Recent Apps or it won't work)

####Expected result:
You should be able to continue editing your messsage
####Actual result:
Textbox gets cleared and everything you did at step 5 is lost
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 6.8.7
####System settings:
Huawei Mate 9 Android 8.0
