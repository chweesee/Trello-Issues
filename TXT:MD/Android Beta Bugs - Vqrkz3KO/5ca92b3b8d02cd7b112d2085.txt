Reported by The Lunala#7777

####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to a group DM. 
- Switch orientation to landscape. 
- Click on the kebab menu on the top right corner. 
- Go to group settings. 
- Click on the leave group button on the top right corner. 
- Click on leave group button in the modal. 
- Immediately switch orientation to portrait.

####Expected result:
You are out of the group.
####Actual result:
You are still in the group. If you try the same thing but by switching to landscape mode, you will get kicked out of the group.
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 870
####System settings:
Lenovo Vibe K5+, 5.1.1
