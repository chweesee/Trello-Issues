####Steps to reproduce:
- Don't have Discord Nitro. 
- Copy four(or any number of) numbers from somewhere.
- Open Settings>My Account>Edit Account. 
- Long press on the tag text field. 
- When the paste option comes, slide your finger away from the text field. 
- After sliding, lift your finger and hit the paste button.

####Expected result:
Either the paste option doesn't come up or the save button doesn't come up after hitting save.
####Actual result:
The paste option comes and when you hit paste, the save button also appears and hitting save says that password is required. When you enter password, you get a toast saying "Account settings successfully updated" (nothing gets updated though).
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 860
####System settings:
Lenovo Vibe K5+, 5.1.1
