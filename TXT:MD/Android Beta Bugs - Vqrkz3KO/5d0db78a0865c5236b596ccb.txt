Reported by Alex1304#9704

####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to a channel where you have both Send messages and Manage channel permissions  
- Write a message over 2000 characters and send it in this channel 
- Once you see the message failing to send, go to Channel settings and make some changes, for example change the channel topic 
- Press Save

####Expected result:
It should only show "Changes saved successfully" toast
####Actual result:
It shows both "Changes saved successfully" and "Cannot exceed 2000 characters" toasts
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 9.0.9
####System settings:
Huawei Mate 9 Android 9.0
