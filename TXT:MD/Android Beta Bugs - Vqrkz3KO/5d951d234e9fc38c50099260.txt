[Archived] [Go Live] Joining and Leaving a Go Live will Disable the Microphone for a noticeable amount of time and cause a AUDIO_RECORD_START_STATE_MISMATCH error in console. (https://trello.com/c/qSZeSUn7)

P4, unlikely to ever be fixed.

Latest Update: 2020-02-20T21:33:52.863Z

Reported by Alipoodle#5025

####Steps to reproduce:
- Have a friend (or alt) on another PC that is live and you can hear their audio 
- Join / Leave a Go Live while talking. 
- Notice how you cannot hear yourself for a few seconds while it loads. 
- Open the Debug Menu (Settings > 3 Dot menu > Debug) and see the error: `WebRtcAudioRecord -> Start recording error: AUDIO_RECORD_START_STATE_MISMATCH.  failed 
- incorrect state :1`

####Expected result:
Your voice is transmitted just fine while Loading / Leaving Go Live
####Actual result:
You are cut off for a Few seconds, More while you leave.
####Client settings:
Discord 9.6.4
####System settings:
OnePlus 3T, Android 9
