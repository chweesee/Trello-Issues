####Steps to reproduce:
- 1) Open the settings page of anything in Discord: it could be server settings, channel settings, category settings, my account settings, or even the create invite menu or any user profile 
- 2) Just press the Home button (do **not** close the app) 
- 3) Go back to Discord

####Expected result:
The Settings menu you opened at step 1 should still be opened
####Actual result:
The menu is closed and you returned to the previous screen, exactly like if you pressed the Back button.
####Client settings:
Discord Android Alpha 5.2.6
####System settings:
Huawei Mate 9 Android 7.0
