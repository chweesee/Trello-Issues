Reported by charleskimbac#6492

####Steps to reproduce:
- Open Discord and turn your status to 'online' or 'do not disturb' 
- Turn off your wifi/data 
- Wait about a minute for Discord to realize that you're offline 
- See that your status is set to invisible in the bottom left corner of your screen 
- Open User Settings 
- See that the statuses do not match

####Expected result:
The status on the Settings page should be set as invisible, matching to one near your username
####Actual result:
Your status on the Settings page doesn't match the one near your username, when it should since you are offline
####Client settings:
Alpha 8.4.4
####System settings:
Samsung Galaxy J7 Prime; Android 7
