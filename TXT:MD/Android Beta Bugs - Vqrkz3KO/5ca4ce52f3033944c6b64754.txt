Latest Update: 2019-04-05T17:06:42.137Z

Reported by Xnehaxixh#2892

####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to Appreance Tab and change your font scale  
- Tap back twice IMMEDIATELY  
- You will see the font has not scaled yet. Exit discord immediately.  
- Open Discord again, it should immediately crash.  
- Open Discord again, it should immediately crash again.  
- [Extended Bug]  
- After changing the font and exit discord immediately  
- Open Discord again, it will crash.  
- The previous step will loop and discord will keep on crashing indefinitely.  
- It will keep on crashing until it's cache is cleared or reinstalled.  
- Specifically mention in the comments of you were able to reproduce the extended bug. 
- Note: Both the bugs might be only reproducible on Android 6 or below. Do comment if you were able to reproduce it on above versions.

####Expected result:
Discord should not crash at all/more than once.
####Actual result:
It crashes twice and in some case indefinitely, making discord unusable.

Discord 869
####System settings:
MIUI 10, Android 6.0.1
