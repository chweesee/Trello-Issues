####Steps to reproduce:
- Ensure you are on the friends/DMs list, then close Discord 
- Reopen Discord 
- Tap on a server on your server list 
- Observe how the Discord logo briefly appears in place of the three humanoid figures 
- __**The steps below are just to confirm that this is a bug and not an intended feature**__

- Observe how the three humanoid figures remain as intended

####Expected result:
The three humanoid figures should always display
####Actual result:
The Discord logo briefly appears, overlapping with the three humanoid figures

Android Alpha 9.0.5
####System settings:
Motorola Moto G6, Android 8.0.0
