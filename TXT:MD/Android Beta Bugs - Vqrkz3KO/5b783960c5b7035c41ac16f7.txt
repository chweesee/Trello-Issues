[Archived] If you jump to a user's DM via his mini profile, minimizing and reopening Discord can reopen the mini profile anywhere in the app (https://trello.com/c/Rawlfoqc)

Reported by Alex1304#9704

####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to any channel 
- Open someone's mini profile 
- Tap "Send a message" option on the mini profile to jump to his DM 
- (optional) Continue using Discord normally: switch channels, etc
- Minimize Discord using Home button 
- Reopen Discord using the shortcut icon (not via multi-tasking)

####Expected result:
You should get back to chat in the same state as you left off
####Actual result:
The mini profile reopens unexpectedly. Depending on what you did at step 4, it may reopen anywhere: in  DMs, in DTesters, in your friends server, or even in your friend list!
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 7.3.8
####System settings:
Huawei Mate 9 Android 8.0
