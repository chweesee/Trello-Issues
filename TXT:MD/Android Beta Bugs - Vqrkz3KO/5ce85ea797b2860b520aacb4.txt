Reported by Nicofisi#4467

####Steps to reproduce:
- Open any channel that has some message history 
- Scroll up until "You're viewing older messages" appears 
- Swipe the screen down very fast and remove your finger so that it will keep scrolling up for a while 
- Click on "You're viewing older messages" while it's still scrolling

####Expected result:
The chat view should instantly move to the newest message and stay there
####Actual result:
It does move but the view doesn't stay there, everything keeps scrolling up
####Client settings:
9.0.3
####System settings:
Huawei P9 Lite 2016, Android 7.0, EMUI 5.0
