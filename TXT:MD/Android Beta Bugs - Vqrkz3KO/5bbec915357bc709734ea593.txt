[Archived] In Quick Switcher, if you search someone whose username has asterisks at the beginning, and the Quick Switcher gives no results, tapping the Add Friend button will paste the username you typed but without the asterisks (https://trello.com/c/nBtbZwJ6)

P3 - Will be fixed eventually,
Low

Created: 2018-10-11T03:52:53.370Z
Latest Update: 2018-11-11T03:00:03.710Z

Reported by Alex1304#9704

####Steps to reproduce:
- Open Quick Switcher 
- Type @ to filter on users only 
- Start typing a username starting with asterisks, and make it so it gives no results. Your search query should look like: `@***test***#0001` 
- In the No Results screen, tap the Add a Friend button 
- Look at the value prefilled in the DiscordTag field

####Expected result:
It should be exactly what you type minus the first @ (which is the filter), in this example `***test***#0001`
####Actual result:
The first asterisks are somehow eaten, so you get only `test***#0001` in the field
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 7.6.6
####System settings:
Huawei Mate 9 Android 8.0
