Reported by Xnehaxixh#2892

####Steps to reproduce:
- Tap and hold and any channel and tap "Edit Channel".  
- Change the channel name to "-" and save it.  
- Then exit from "Channel Settings" [This is a necessary step to reproduce this bug].  
- Tap and hold on the same channel and tap "Edit Channel".  
- Rename the channel to something else, say "discord" and tap save.

####Expected result:
The channel name displayed under "Edit Channel" should get updated to the new name.
####Actual result:
The channel name under the "Edit Channel" completely disappears.
####Client settings:
Discord 8.4.5g
####System settings:
Xiaomi Redmi 3S Prime, MIUI 10, Android 6.0.1
