####Steps to reproduce:
- Have a Spotify account connected to discord  
- Play a song on Spotify and wait for discord to detect it  
- Play 3 to 4 songs without skipping  
- Pause the song and go to another app (preferably YouTube) make sure you don't swipe discord or  Spotify away from recent apps 
- Use the other app for sometime  (untill your OS automatically closes discord due to inactivity, might take upto half an hour 
- Now go to Spotify and search a new song and play it  
- Go back to discord via recent apps

####Expected result:
You should be able to use discord while listening to music
####Actual result:
App crashes badly
####Client settings:
Alpha 8.2.4
####System settings:
ASUS Zenfone max pro m1 Android 8.1.0
