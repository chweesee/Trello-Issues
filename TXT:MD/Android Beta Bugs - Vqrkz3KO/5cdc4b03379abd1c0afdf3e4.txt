If you scroll up/down briefly in channel list of a server with a banner, and then switch to a server without banner, it will be difficult to scroll the channel list of that server (https://trello.com/c/GI2fVlJ2)

None

Created: 2019-05-15T17:23:15.757Z
Latest Update: 2020-04-13T06:16:09.835Z

Reported by Alex1304#9704

####Steps to reproduce:
- Switch to a server with a banner, e.g Discord Testers 
- Scroll down and then scroll up very briefly through the channel list (the scroll distance must be very short, and also you must release your finger before scrolling back up)  
- Switch to a different server without banner, but with a lot of channels 
- Try to scroll the channel list (without using inertia). It will be difficult because it auto scrolls you up. 
- Might take a couple of tries, also tjose repro steps aren't strict, there must be other ways to trigger it as it happens quite randomly in normal conditions for me

####Expected result:
The channel list should always be easy to scroll
####Actual result:
The channel list scrolling is difficult and unsmooth
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 8.9.8
####System settings:
Huawei Mate 9 Android 9.0
