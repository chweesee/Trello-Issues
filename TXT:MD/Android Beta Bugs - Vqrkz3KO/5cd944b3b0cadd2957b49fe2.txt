####Steps to reproduce:
- Find an embed which has a Description that ends with a link. You can test it with the !search command of MegaBot in DFeedback 
- Long press on the description. Make sure to wait for the toast "Copied to clipboard" 
- Move your finger up or down to scroll the screen a bit, then release it.

####Expected result:
After releasing your finger the highlight should be removed
####Actual result:
The long-press highlight gets stuck

Discord Alpha 8.9.6
####System settings:
Huawei Mate 9 Android 9.0
