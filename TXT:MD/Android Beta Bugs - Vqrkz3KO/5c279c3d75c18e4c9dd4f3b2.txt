####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to user settings. 
- Go to my account. 
- Ensure you have a custom profile picture set. 
- Ensure you have more than one photo viewing app, example on Samsung, gallery and Google Photos. 
- Ensure you haven't any default photo viewing/choosing apps set. 
- Remove your current set profile picture but don't apply/save changes. 
- Click that default profile picture to set a custom one. 
- When asked to choose your photo choosing app, click the back button or anywhere outside of the space they ask you on.

####Expected result:
You should still have that default picture chosen.
####Actual result:
You go back to your old profile picture.
####Client settings:
Android Alpha 8.2.2
####System settings:
Samsung Galaxy Tab A, Android 7.1.1
