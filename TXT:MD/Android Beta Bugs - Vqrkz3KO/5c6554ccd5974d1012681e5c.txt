####Steps to reproduce:
- Log out of your account.  
- Tap on "REGISTER".  
- Fill in the box and fail the registration.  
- [Here's an easy way to fail the registration: try to register with an existing email.]  
- Tap "REGISTER" and fail the registration.  
- Go back and log into your account.

####Expected result:
After logging in, it should take you directly to your FRIENDS/SERVER list.
####Actual result:
Instead, you reach this screen: "Do you have an Invite?". This page was intended for newly registered users and not for existing users.
####Client settings:
Discord 8.4.4g
####System settings:
Xiaomi Redmi 3S Prime, MIUI 10, Android 6.0.1
