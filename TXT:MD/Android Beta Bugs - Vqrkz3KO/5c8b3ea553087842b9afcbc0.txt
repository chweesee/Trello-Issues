####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to notification settings of any server. 
- Select notification overrides for any two channels. 
- In the notification settings page, scroll down. 
- Slide one channel/category override. 
- While the place where category/channel override still is red with the trash bin icon, slide the second channel/category override. 
- Observe as the red placeholder for the first category/channel override vanishes.

####Expected result:
Nothing out of ordinary.
####Actual result:
The second override (which was already sliden away) does the sliding animation again.
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 856
####System settings:
Lenovo Vibe K5+, 5.1.1
