####Steps to reproduce:
- Disable 2FA if you have it enabled. 
- Go to Settings>My Account>Enable 2FA. 
- Do as you are asked to. 
- In the second step, after entering the key in authy/google authenticator app, don't copy the code. 
- Go back to Discord. 
- Open keyboard. 
- Type the code manually.

####Expected result:
The keyboard closes after 2FA is successfully enabled.
####Actual result:
The keyboard remains open even after 2FA has successfully been enabled.

Discord Alpha 856
####System settings:
Lenovo Vibe K5+, 5.1.1
