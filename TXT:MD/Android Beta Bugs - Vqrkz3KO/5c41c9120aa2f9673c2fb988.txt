####Steps to reproduce:
- Find/upload a gif to Discord. 
- Let someone add a reaction to the gif. You can also add or remove a reaction. 
- Observe how the gif starts from the beginning again. (Also seems to happen with animated emotes)

####Expected result:
The gif should continue playing while reactions are being added to it or removed.
####Actual result:
The gif reloads when you or someone else reacts to the gif or remove a reaction.
