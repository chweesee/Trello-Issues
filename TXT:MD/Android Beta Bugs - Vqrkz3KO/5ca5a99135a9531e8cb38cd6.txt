####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to Settings>Private & Safety. 
- Scroll to bottom and click on the header "Use data to make Discord work" header. 
- After you are redirected to the "My Account" page and the Disable Account shows the clicking hint twice, switch orientation.

####Expected result:
Nothing out of ordinary.
####Actual result:
The Disable Account shows the clicking hint again. If you switch orientation again, it shows the clicking hint again. If you keep doing it for like 1000 times, it'll show the hint 1000 times.
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 870
####System settings:
Lenovo Vibe K5+, 5.1.1
