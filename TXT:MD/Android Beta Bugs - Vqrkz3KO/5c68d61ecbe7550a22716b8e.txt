[Archived] When you login to a non-nitro account and then login to a Nitro account and type colons (:) in the message box, the emoji from the previous account are also listed (works on both sides with global emoji). (https://trello.com/c/FZsb6LU3)

P4, unlikely to ever be fixed.

Created: 2019-02-17T03:33:50.421Z
Latest Update: 2019-06-05T23:46:26.001Z

Reported by The Lunala#7777

####Steps to reproduce:
- Logout and login with a non Nitro account. 
- Use some emoji. 
- Logout and login with a Nitro account. 
- Open the emoji picker. 
- Look in recently used emoji. 
- Alternate steps: 
- After logging in with the Nitro account, type in colons in the message box. 
- Observe that the emoji from the previous account listed.

####Expected result:
The emoji don't get synced across accounts.
####Actual result:
The emoji get synced across accounts. If you don't have Nitro, you can reproduce this using global emoji.
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 846
####System settings:
Lenovo Vibe K5+, 5.1.1
