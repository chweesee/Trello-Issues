Reported by MobButcher#5874

####Steps to reproduce:
- Search for a message with built-in search. 
- Tap on it so it will send you to the chat history. 
- Scroll the chat up and down.

####Expected result:
Only the message that was searched for is highlighted. (Shifted to the left and blinked out in blue color.)
####Actual result:
Other messages send from another users and in other chats, servers or DMs get highlighted as well. It affects almost all messages, even those which are in the middle of grouped ones, send by a user is a short while.
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 5.8.7
####System settings:
LG G60 X135 @ Android 4.4.2
