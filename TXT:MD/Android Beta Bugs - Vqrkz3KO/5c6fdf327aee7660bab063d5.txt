####Steps to reproduce:
- In your device settings, set your multitasking  button (the hardware button other than the back and home button) to open recent apps on long press. 
- Open Discord. 
- Now open Settings>My Account>Enable 2FA. 
- Do as you're directed to. 
- In the page where you enter 2FA code, enter a wrong code. 
- Now, click your multitasking button just once.

####Expected result:
Nothing happens.
####Actual result:
Discord reconfirms the code.
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 849
####System settings:
Lenovo Vibe K5+, 5.1.1
