####Steps to reproduce:
- Go to a server where you can edit your highest role 
- Make sure your highest role has a role color 
- Make your status invisible 
- Go to Server settings -> Roles -> Your highest Role -> Change color of the role 
- Go to any channel of the server and click the 'Members' button and Observe it shows old role color of your highest role on your name

It will show the new role color on your name at member list if you are invisible/offline
####Actual result:
It shows the role color of your highest role before you edit the role's color if you are invisible/offline

Discord Alpha 8.5.0
