####Steps to reproduce:
- Pick any text channel. Also works with DMs 
- Read messages in it, or mark it as Read 
- exit out of the channel 
- have someone post a new message in that channel and then ask him to delete it 
- return to the channel 
- send a message

####Expected result:
Your message will be sent, and nothing else will appear
####Actual result:
Your message will be sent, but the New messages red separator will appear above your message

Discord Alpha 5.6.6
####System settings:
Huawei Mate 9 Android 7.0
