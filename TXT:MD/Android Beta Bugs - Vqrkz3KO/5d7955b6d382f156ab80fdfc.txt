####Steps to reproduce:
- Write a long message 
- Place your cursor in the middle or at the start of the message 
- Start typing 
- Get someone to send you a friend request  
- As you receive the in-app notification about the friend request sent, continue typing

####Expected result:
You should be continuing typing without interruption
####Actual result:
The cursor jumps to the end of the message, causing what you're typing to be out of place

Discord Alpha 9.5.3
####System settings:
Huawei Mate 9 Android 9.0
