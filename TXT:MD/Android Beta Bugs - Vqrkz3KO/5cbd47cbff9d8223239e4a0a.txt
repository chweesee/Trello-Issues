Reported by The Lunala#7882

####Steps to reproduce:
- Set slowmode in a channel (or just ask someone to constantly keep typing). 
- When the typing indicator appears, scroll up in the chat and hide a mention just behind the typing indicator (i.e. the typing indicator is above a mention. The mention could be either a channel mention or a user mention). 
- Click on the place on the typing indicator behind which there's the mention (For ex: If the mention is on the extreme right of a message then hide the mention with the typing indicator and click on the extreme right behind which there's the mention).

####Expected result:
The messages behind the typing indicator don't react the click.
####Actual result:
They do react to the click and the DM with the mentioned user opens. This can also be reproduced with reactions, links, etc.
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 880
####System settings:
Lenovo Vibe K5+, 5.1.1
