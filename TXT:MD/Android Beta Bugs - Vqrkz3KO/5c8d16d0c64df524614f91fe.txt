[Archived] Languages that follow a DD MM YYYY date format don't display the expiry date of Nitro correctly. Not a translation issue because it displays fine on Desktop. (https://trello.com/c/ocwSp7iL)

None

Created: 2019-03-16T15:31:28.369Z
Latest Update: 2019-06-05T23:46:21.874Z

Reported by Alex1304#9704

####Steps to reproduce:
- Have an ongoing Nitro subscription that you haven't cancelled. Make sure that you don't have any payment method configured to renew your Nitro automatically (otherwise a different string will be shown) 
- Switch to a language that uses the date format DD MM YYYY. Example: French 
- Go to Settings > Nitro and observe expiry date of Nitro

####Expected result:
It should use the correct format, in my case `26 mars 2019`
####Actual result:
It uses the incorrect format, in my case `mars 26 2019`. This bug does not exist on Desktop.
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 8.5.9
####System settings:
Huawei Mate 9 Android 8.0
