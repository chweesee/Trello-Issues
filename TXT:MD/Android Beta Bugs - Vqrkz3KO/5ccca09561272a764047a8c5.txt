None

Created: 2019-05-03T20:12:05.712Z
Latest Update: 2019-08-16T21:25:12.988Z

Reported by Alex1304#9704

####Steps to reproduce:
- To test this bug in optimal conditions, do it in a channel with no activity at all and that has no unreads already 
- Make sure you're out of the channel 
- Have someone send a message in the channel 
- Have him delete the message immediately 
- Once you have ensured that the message is deleted, you can switch to the channel 
- Switch to a different channel. Observe how the channel wasn't parked as read

####Expected result:
The channel should be read
####Actual result:
The channel isn't read and you need to Mark as read the channel manually
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 8.8.9
####System settings:
Huawei Mate 9 Android 9.0
