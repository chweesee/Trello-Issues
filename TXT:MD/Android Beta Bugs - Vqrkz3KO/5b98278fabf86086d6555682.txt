####Steps to reproduce:
- Have a poor connection that is enough to show you the connection problems in app notification. 
- Close Discord but keep it running in background. 
- Open it after it has disconnected itself(not killed. Just disconnected). 
- While it's connecting, click on search and search something that gives results. 
- Stay on the results page for a while. 
- When Discord has reconnected, close the search.

####Expected result:
The connection problems in app notification doesn't show because you have successfully connected.
####Actual result:
The in app notification still shows up.
####Client settings:
Discord Alpha 748
####System settings:
Lenovo vibe K5+, 5.1.1
