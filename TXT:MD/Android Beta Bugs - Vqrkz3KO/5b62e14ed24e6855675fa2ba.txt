####Steps to reproduce:
- Create a server. 
- Long press on the general voice channel. 
- Click on edit channel. 
- Make changes to the bitrate and/or the user limit values (do not save them). 
- Scroll down and click on permissions. 
- Click on back. 
- Observe the bitrate and user limit values.

####Expected result:
Your changes are still there.
####Actual result:
The values reset themselves which shouldn't happen because you haven't yet left the settings.
