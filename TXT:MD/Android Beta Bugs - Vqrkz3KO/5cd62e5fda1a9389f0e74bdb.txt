Reported by Bullseye#8200

####Steps to reproduce:
- Paste an embed link into the chat. (E.g: facebook, or a youtube video link, etc). 
- Long-press until the action modal opens. 
- Long-press in the rectangle where the link is underlined. 
- Swipe up.

####Expected result:
Nothing happens because you have swiped away. And swiping away normally does not trigger the button that you were pressing, or the link that you were long-pressing.
####Actual result:
You get redirected to the linked page, video, etc.
####Client settings:
Android Alpha 8.9.4
####System settings:
Huawei Y7, Android 8.0.0
