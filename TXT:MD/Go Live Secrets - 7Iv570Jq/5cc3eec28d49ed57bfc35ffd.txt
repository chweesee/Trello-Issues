**Short Description**: Quickly hovering between multiple people streaming causes cards to overlap temporarily
**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Find a server where 2 or more people are streaming
2. Quickly hover between them
3. Observe how the cards overlap temporarily
**Expected Result:** The cards go away right after you stop hovering over them
**Actual Result:** Cards begin to overlap each other temporarily
**Client Settings:** Canary 36518
**System Settings:** Windows 10 Home x64 (1809)
