None

Latest Update: 2019-04-27T14:30:08.098Z

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Have two people begin a "live" session in different channels positioned near each other.
2. Quickly move your mouse between the two users.

**Expected Result:** The popout disappears in time for the next one to appear.

**Actual Result:** The popout stays on screen a little longer than desirable causing a weird effect.

**Client Settings:** Canary 36525

**System Settings:** MacBook Pro (13", Mid-2012), macOS v10.14.4 (18E226)
