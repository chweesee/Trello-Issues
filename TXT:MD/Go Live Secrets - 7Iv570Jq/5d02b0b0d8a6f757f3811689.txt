Streaming Microsoft Store apps prompts for admin perms, then has no audio. (https://trello.com/c/bPLzIcNU)

None

Created: 2019-06-13T20:23:12.964Z
Latest Update: 2019-06-13T21:19:33.642Z

**Steps to reproduce:**
- Launch Discord as admin
- When client appears, launch a Microsoft store app
- Go live, experience https://trello.com/c/M5EPuPFf
- Stop streaming
- Wait 4-5 minutes before going live again, and confirm
**Expected Results:** You should go live, without being prompted for admin.
**Actual Results:** You will be prompted for admin in-client, to enable sound, and even when granted, there will be no sound, with a yellow indicator in the top-right of the preview.
**System Settings:** Windows 10 Pro x64 (1809) (DxDiag attached from SoT), tested with Sea of Thieves and Fallout Shelter
**Client Settings:** Canary 39636
