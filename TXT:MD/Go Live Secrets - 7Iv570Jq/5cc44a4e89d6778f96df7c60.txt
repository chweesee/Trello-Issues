The "live" popout won't disappear once a session ends. (https://trello.com/c/I5Kw1dwT)

None

Created: 2019-04-27T12:25:50.673Z
Latest Update: 2019-06-17T22:08:20.291Z

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Have someone begin a "live" session.
2. Hover over their user with your cursor to display the "live" popout.
3. Have them end the session.

**Expected Result:** The popout instantly disappears.

**Actual Result:** The popout stays on screen until you hover away.

**Client Settings:** Canary 36525

**System Settings:** MacBook Pro (13", Mid-2012), macOS v10.14.4 (18E226)
