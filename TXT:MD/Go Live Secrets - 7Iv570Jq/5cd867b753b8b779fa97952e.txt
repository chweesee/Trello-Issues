**Short Description:**
Stream Invite embed still says person is still streaming even if person isn't streaming.
**Steps to reproduce:**
- Make an invite to someone's stream.
- Paste it in chat.
- Wait for stream to end and the streamer to leave the channel.

**Expected Results:**
Invite embed would not show that streamer's still streaming.
**Actual Results:**
Invite embed still shows the streamer's still streaming. Even if you repost the invite, it'll still say streamer's streaming.
**System Settings:** Windows 10 Home x64
**Client Settings:** Canary 37458
