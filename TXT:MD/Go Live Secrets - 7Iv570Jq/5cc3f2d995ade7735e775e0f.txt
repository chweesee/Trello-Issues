**Short Description:** When in a different vc than a person streaming and then attempting to watch their stream it will auto join you to the vc but will not automatically open the stream
**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Be in a different vc than someone streaming (this will not work if you are currently watching a stream)
2. Hover over than name and click watch stream
3. Observe how you join the vc but the stream doesn't start
**Expected Result:** The stream starts
**Actual Result:** The stream doesn't start 
**Client Settings:** Canary 36518
**System Settings:** Windows 10 Home x64 (1809)
