Stream settings maintain quality and FPS settings from localstorage even if the user does not have Nitro (https://trello.com/c/Y593Enoj)

None

Created: 2019-06-14T01:07:07.708Z
Latest Update: 2019-06-17T22:03:58.117Z

**Steps to reproduce:**
- Have an account with Nitro and an account without Nitro
- Start a stream on the account with Nitro and go to stream settings and select source.
- Sign out and start a stream on the account without Nitro on the same client.
- Open stream settings.
**Expected Results:** As the account does not have Nitro, it should not show the Nitro settings being used.
**Actual Results:** It shows the Nitro settings being used and the Nitro text is invisible.
**System Settings:** Windows 10 Pro x64 v1903 18912.1001  
**Client Settings:** Canary 39690
