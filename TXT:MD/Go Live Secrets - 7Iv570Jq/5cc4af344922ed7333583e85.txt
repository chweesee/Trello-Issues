Posting a stream invite in chat says that you are live even if someone else is live. (https://trello.com/c/m3vqhhJK)

Investigating

Created: 2019-04-27T19:36:20.587Z
Latest Update: 2019-06-17T22:08:20.499Z

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Have someone go live in a server.
2. Watch the stream.
3. Make a stream invite by inviting through the popup in the Discord app.
4. Post it in chat.

**Expected Result:** The embed correctly says who's live.

**Actual Result:** The embed says that you (the current user) is live.

**Client Settings:** Canary 36526

**System Settings:** Windows 10 Home x64 (1809)
