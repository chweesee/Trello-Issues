**Short Description:** When watching a stream on iOS in landscape, then changing your theme on desktop, the orientation of the screen turns vertical.
**Steps to reproduce:**
- Watch a stream on iOS.
- Turn your device horizontally so the orientation becomes landscape.
- On desktop, toggle your theme.
**Expected Results:** The stream stays in landscape.
**Actual Results:** The stream turns portrait.
**System Settings:** MacBook Pro (13", Mid-2012), macOS v10.14.5 (18F132) - iPhone 6s, iOS 13.0 (17A5492t)
**Client Settings:** Canary 39806 - TestFlight 3.0.3 (15738)
