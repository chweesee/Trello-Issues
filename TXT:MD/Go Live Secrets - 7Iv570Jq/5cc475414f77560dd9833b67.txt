**Short Description:** You can change audio level without the audio popup
**Steps to reproduce:**
1. Watch someone streaming
2. Double click on them to open theater mode
3. Open the audio menu and drag the audio menu
4. While dragging go to the left or right of the menu
5. Observe how the menu closes yet you can still change the audio level
**Expected Result:** The audio menu should stay open as you are modifying the audio level
**Actual Result:** The audio menu closes but you are still able to modify the level
**Client Settings:** Canary 36525
**System Settings:** Windows 10 Home x64 (1809)
