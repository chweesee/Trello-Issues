**Short Description:**
Clicking on Invite or User Settings in full screen opens the modal behind the stream.
**Steps to reproduce:**
- Watch a stream.
- Click "Full Screen" on bottom right.
- Click Invite and/or User Settings.
- Nothing happens.
- Click "Full Screen" on bottom right again to exit full screen.

**Expected Results:**
The stream exits full screen.
**Actual Results:**
You're greeted by the Invite modal or the User Settings page depending on which you click.
**System Settings:** Windows 10 Home x64
**Client Settings:** Canary 36518
