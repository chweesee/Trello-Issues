**Short Description:** When you reload your client and someone is streaming the stream start sound will play once the client has loaded
**Steps to reproduce:**
1. Watch someone streaming
3. Use control + r to reload discord
4. Observe how once the client loads back up the stream start sound plays
**Expected Result:** No sound plays like when you join/leave the channel with someone streaming
**Actual Result:** The stream start sound plays once the client has fully loaded back up
**Client Settings:** Canary 36529
**System Settings:** Windows 10 Home x64 (1809)
