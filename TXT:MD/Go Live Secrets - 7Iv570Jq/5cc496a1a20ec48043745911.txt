Latest Update: 2019-06-17T22:03:22.738Z

**Short Description:** Profile pictures with transparency show other profile pictures through their circles when fullscreened
**Steps to reproduce:**
- Have a couple users with profile pictures with transparency
- Enlarge the streaming window
- Hover the window to bring the profile pictures up
**Expected Results:** Transparency has no effect on the profile picture circles
**Actual Results:** Transparent profile pictures cause profile pictures behind them to show within their circle
**System Settings:** Canary Build 36525 Host 0.0.235
**Client Settings:** Windows 10 Home x64 1809
