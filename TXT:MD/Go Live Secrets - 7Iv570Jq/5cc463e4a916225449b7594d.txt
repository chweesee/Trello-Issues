####Short Description: 
Video preview in corner overlaps the stream when in theater mode
####Steps to reproduce:
 - Find someone streaming
 - Click on them 4 times so the stream goes into theater mode
 - Go into another server and wait for the small video to appear in the corner
 - Go back to the server where the livestream is happening 
 - Observe how the small video overlaps the larger video when in theater mode temporarily

####Expected Results: 
The preview does not overlap the larger video
####Actual Results:
The preview overlaps the larger video temporarily
####Client Settings: 
Canary 36525
####System Settings: 
Windows 10 Home x64 (1809)
