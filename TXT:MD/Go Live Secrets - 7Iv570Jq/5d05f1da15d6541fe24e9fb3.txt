**Short Description:** You can select the Watch Stream button multiple times on iOS.
**Steps to reproduce:**
- Watch a stream on iOS.
- Tap on the streaming user's profile (from members list or something.)
- Tap "Watch Stream."
**Expected Results:** The button is disabled saying "Watching Stream."
**Actual Results:** You're able to select the button when you're already watching the stream.
**System Settings:** iPhone 6s, iOS 13.0 (17A5492t)
**Client Settings:** TestFlight 3.0.4 (15811)
