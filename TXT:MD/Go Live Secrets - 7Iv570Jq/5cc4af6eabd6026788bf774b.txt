**Short Description:** When you CTRL + R a popout window an error appears in the console
**Steps to reproduce:**
- Open dev tools in the main window and go to the console tab
- Join a stream and open a popout
- Ctrl + R that window
**Expected Result:** There should be no error in the console and the popout should reopen.
**Actual Result:** The popout closes with an error in the console and does not reopen.
**System Settings:** Windows 10 Pro x64 v1809 Build 18875.1000 
**Client Settings:** Canary 36526
