**Short Description: **Stop streaming button shows end after the stream has ended
**Steps to reproduce:**
1. Start streaming
2. Move channels
3. Observe how the preview in the corner of your screen says "This stream has ended"
4. Hover over the monitor with an x icon about the settings cog
5. Observe how it says stop streaming even though you already have stopped
**Expected Result:** The button should go back to the button where you hit it to start streaming
**Actual Result:** The button is the stop streaming button
