**Short Description:** Left clicking a spectator in the popout player causes the spectator list to stay open until you close and reopen the popout player
**Steps to reproduce:**
1. Watch someone's stream 
2. Open the popout player
3. Hover over a spectator profile picture so the spectator menu opens
4. Left click one of the spectators
5. Stop hovering over the spectator menu
6. Observe how the menu is now stuck open
**Expected Result:** The spectator menu closes
**Actual Result:** The spectator menu stays open and can not be closed with out closing the popout player and reopening it
**Client Settings:** Canary 36529
**System Settings:** Windows 10 Home x64 (1809)
