**Short Description:** Volume bar resets everytime you go between the small popup and the theater mode window
**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Double click someone streaming
2. Click them another 2 times so you can see the stream in a bigger window and see the other options
3. Change the volume
4. Go to text channel
5. Double click the streamer 2 times again, observe how the volume slider has reset
**Expected Result:** The slider stays where it was last at
**Actual Result:** The slider resets
**Client Settings:** Canary 36518
**System Settings:** Windows 10 Home x64 (1809)
